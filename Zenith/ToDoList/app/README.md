🌌 ZENITH: The Ultimate Progress Engine
"Visualizing growth. Engineering discipline."

Zenith is a high-performance Android application designed to bridge the gap between intent and action.
Built with a modern Kotlin/Jetpack Compose frontend, it offers a cinematic approach to personal progress monitoring.
With special features like, percentage bar, in app and in notification bar also, user will select, alarm , timer or both, here alarm will
do both things, like user will use it as an alarm for a specific time, or as a deadline for his, task, its like "I will study 2 hours
before 9 pm" here 9 pm is his deadline, the percentage bar will stop, when he press done or the alarm ring, the percentage of 
work which he done will show on percentage bar like, 30% 50% or 100%.
